Our Story

"I with my father opened Hungry Hunter in June 2005 after we travelled to diffrent states in India and fell in love with the food."

"We made the decision to start a restaurant serving food while sat on high stools, and talking with my father."

"15 years later, Hungry Hunter is still a family-run business serving great value, healthy Indian street food that is cooked fresh daily."
